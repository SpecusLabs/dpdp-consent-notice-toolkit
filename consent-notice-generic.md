## How to Use This Template

1. Fill in every [SQUARE BRACKET] field
2. Remove any data categories or purposes that do not apply to your business
3. Add any data categories or purposes that are missing
4. Place this notice at the point of data collection — before asking the user to consent
5. Link to your full privacy policy from this notice
6. Have a qualified legal professional review before going live
