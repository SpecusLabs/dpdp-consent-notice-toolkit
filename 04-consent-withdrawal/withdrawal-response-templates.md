# Withdrawal Response Templates

**Email and written response templates for every stage of a data rights request — acknowledgement through completion.**

> **Disclaimer:** This is not legal advice. Review with a qualified professional before use.
> All fields in [SQUARE BRACKETS] must be filled in before use.

---

## How to Use These Templates

There are four templates in this file:

1. **Acknowledgement** — Send within 48 hours of receiving any request
2. **Completion — Erasure / Withdrawal** — Send when deletion is done
3. **Completion — Access** — Send when you've compiled the data copy
4. **Completion — Grievance** — Send at the end of a grievance investigation

Each template has a subject line, email body, and notes on what to customise.

---

## Template 1 — Acknowledgement (All Request Types)

Send this within 48 hours of receiving any data rights request.

---

**Subject:** We've received your data request — Ref: [REFERENCE NUMBER]

---

Dear [NAME],

Thank you for contacting us. We have received your [data access / correction / erasure / grievance] request, submitted on [DATE].

**Your reference number is: [REFERENCE NUMBER]**

Please keep this reference number — you will need it if you contact us about this request.

**What happens next:**

We will review your request and respond within [7 working days]. If we need any additional information from you to process your request, we will contact you at this email address.

**What your request covers:**

You have asked us to [briefly describe what they asked for — e.g., delete your personal data and withdraw your consent / provide a copy of the data we hold about you / correct your address in our records].

We will confirm exactly what we have done once your request is processed.

**Questions?**

If you have any questions in the meantime, contact our Grievance Officer:

[GRIEVANCE OFFICER NAME]
[GRIEVANCE OFFICER EMAIL]
[PHONE — optional]

If your concern is not resolved by us, you may also contact the **Data Protection Board of India**.

Regards,
[YOUR NAME]
[DESIGNATION]
[COMPANY NAME]

## Template 2 — Completion: Erasure and Withdrawal

Send this when you have completed the deletion and notified all vendors.

---

**Subject:** Your data has been deleted — Ref: [REFERENCE NUMBER]

---

Dear [NAME],

We are writing to confirm that we have completed your erasure and consent withdrawal request (Ref: [REFERENCE NUMBER], received on [DATE]).

**What we have deleted:**

We have deleted the following data from our systems:

- [e.g., Your name, email address, and phone number from our customer database]
- [e.g., Your order history and browsing data]
- [e.g., Your email address from our marketing lists]
- [e.g., Your profile from our worker registration system]
- [List every category deleted]

**What we have retained (and why):**

The following data has been retained because we are required to keep it under Indian law:

| Data | Reason for Retention | Retention Period |
|------|---------------------|-----------------|
| [e.g., Transaction records for orders placed] | GST Act and Income Tax Act — mandatory 7-year retention | Until [YEAR] |
| [e.g., PF and ESIC records] | EPFO / ESIC regulations | Until [YEAR] |
| [e.g., Wage payment records] | Payment of Wages Act | Until [YEAR] |

This data is held in restricted-access storage and will not be used for any purpose other than statutory compliance. It will be permanently deleted on [DATE].

**Vendors notified:**

We have notified the following third parties who held a copy of your data, and requested that they delete it:

| Vendor | Date Notified | Confirmed Deletion |
|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| [VENDOR NAME — e.g., Delhivery] | [DATE] | [Yes / Pending] |
| [VENDOR NAME — e.g., Mailchimp] | [DATE] | [Yes / Pending] |
| [VENDOR NAME] | [DATE] | [Yes / Pending] |

**Your consent has been withdrawn:**

We have formally recorded the withdrawal of your consent. We will no longer use your personal data for any of the purposes you originally consented to.

If you have any questions about this confirmation, contact:

[GRIEVANCE OFFICER NAME]

## Template 4 — Completion: Grievance

Send this at the conclusion of a grievance investigation.

---

**Subject:** Update on your grievance — Ref: [REFERENCE NUMBER]

---

Dear [NAME],

We are writing to provide an update on your grievance (Ref: [REFERENCE NUMBER], received on [DATE]).

**Your grievance:**

You raised a concern about [brief description of what they complained about — e.g., the way your Aadhaar data was shared with a client company without your knowledge / the difficulty of withdrawing consent from our platform].

**Our investigation:**

We investigated your concern and found the following:

[Describe what you investigated — e.g., We reviewed our records of when your Aadhaar number was shared with [CLIENT NAME] and found that it was shared on [DATE] as part of your placement at their facility.]

**Our findings:**

[Choose one and adapt:]

*If you found a process failure:*
> We found that [describe what went wrong]. This should not have happened. We have [describe remedial action — e.g., corrected the record / notified the third party to delete the data / updated our internal process to prevent recurrence].

*If the process was followed correctly:*
> We found that [describe what you found]. Your data was [e.g., shared with [VENDOR] as disclosed in the consent notice you signed on [DATE], for the purpose of [PURPOSE]]. This was consistent with the consent you provided. We understand this may not be the outcome you were hoping for.

**What we have done:**

- [Action 1 — e.g., Notified [VENDOR] to delete the relevant data]
- [Action 2 — e.g., Corrected our internal records]
- [Action 3 — e.g., Updated our process to require written confirmation before sharing data with client companies]

**If you are not satisfied:**

If you are not satisfied with this outcome, you have the right to file a complaint with the **Data Protection Board of India**.

We are sorry for any distress this situation has caused.

Contact our Grievance Officer if you have further questions:
[GRIEVANCE OFFICER NAME]
[GRIEVANCE OFFICER EMAIL]

Regards,
[YOUR NAME]
[DESIGNATION]
[COMPANY NAME]
