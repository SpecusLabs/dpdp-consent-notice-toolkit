## [1.0.0] — 2024-01-01

### Added
- Initial release of the DPDP Consent Notice Kit
- `01-dpdp-reference/` — Plain English explanations of DPDP Act Sections 5, 6, and 7
  - `consent-sections-explained.md` — Overview of all consent-related sections
  - `section-5-notice-requirements.md` — Detailed breakdown of Section 5
  - `section-6-consent-requirements.md` — Detailed breakdown of Section 6
  - `section-7-legitimate-use.md` — Detailed breakdown of Section 7
- `02-consent-notice-templates/` — Ready-to-use consent notice templates
  - Generic consent notice (`.md` and `.html`)
  - D2C / e-commerce consent notice (`.md` and `.html`)
  - Labour / staffing consent notice (`.md` and `.html`)
  - AI SaaS consent notice (`.md` and `.html`)
  - Offline / paper consent notice (`.md` and print-optimised `.html`)
- `03-privacy-policy-templates/` — Privacy policy frameworks by sector
  - Generic privacy policy
  - D2C / e-commerce privacy policy
  - Labour / staffing privacy policy
  - AI SaaS privacy policy
- `04-consent-withdrawal/` — End-to-end withdrawal handling
  - Withdrawal process guide
  - Withdrawal request form (`.md` and embeddable `.html`)
  - Withdrawal response templates
  - Withdrawal log tracker (`.md` and `.csv`)
  - Withdrawal vendor notification template
- `05-sector-guides/` — Consolidated implementation guides
  - D2C / e-commerce guide
  - Labour / staffing guide
  - AI SaaS guide

### Notes
- DPDP Rules have not yet been finalised by the Government of India as of this release
- All templates will be updated when Rules are published
- Review with a qualified legal professional before use
