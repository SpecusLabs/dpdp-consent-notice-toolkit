## 8. Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

**Right to Access** — You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will respond within [TIMEFRAME — e.g., 7 working days].

**Right to Correction** — You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

**Right to Erasure** — You can ask us to delete your personal data. We will comply subject to any legal retention requirements. For example, we must keep transaction records for tax purposes, but we will delete all other data.

**Right to Withdraw Consent** — Where we process your data based on consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before the withdrawal.

**Right to Grievance** — If you believe your data rights have been violated, you can file a grievance with our Grievance Officer (see Section 9 below).

**Right to Complain to the Board** — If your grievance is not resolved, you can file a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India.
