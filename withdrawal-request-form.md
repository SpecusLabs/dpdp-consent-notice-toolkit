# Withdrawal / Rights Request Form

**Give this form to any person who wants to withdraw consent, access their data, request correction, or file a grievance.**

> **Disclaimer:** This is not legal advice. Review with a qualified professional before use.

---

## How to Use This Form

**For digital delivery:** Share the link to `withdrawal-request-form.html` — it is a web form that can be embedded on your website or sent directly.

**For physical delivery:** Print this page and give it to the person making the request. They complete and sign it; you retain the signed original.

**Processing:** Once you receive a completed form, follow the steps in `withdrawal-process-guide.md`.
