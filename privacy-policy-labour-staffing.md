## 8. Worker Rights

**Know and access:** Workers can request a copy of their data by contacting the Grievance Officer. We will respond within [TIMEFRAME — e.g., 7 working days].

**Correct:** Workers can request correction of any inaccurate data (e.g., wrong bank account number, incorrect date of birth in records).

**Withdraw consent:** Workers can withdraw consent for data collected on a consent basis (skills profile, work history sharing with clients). Note: statutory data (PAN, Aadhaar, bank details) cannot be withdrawn while the worker is engaged with us, as it is collected under legal obligation.

**Erase:** Workers can request erasure of non-statutory data. Statutory records (PF, ESIC, TDS, wage records) must be retained for the periods specified above.

**Grievance:** Workers can file a grievance with our Grievance Officer (Section 10).

**Complain to the Board:** If the grievance is not resolved, the worker may complain to the Data Protection Board of India.
